PCR and The Diagnosis of Infectious Diseases

Zoom in to a macroscopic scale!


Learn more about Polymerase Chain Reaction

Come meet Lisbon with us!


    Let me talk you about the biggest smallest thing in the world. Yes you heard it right, it´s something that even with the most tiny of the dimensions, as a whole universe of information and complexity encrypted in its structure, and it´s called DNA.
    Now imagine that you could amplified that structure just like when you zoom a picture in your phone: that´s what PCR is. PCR stands for Polymerase Chain Reaction, and it allows the amplification of specific fragments of DNA- DNA Targets- leading to the production of millions of copies of that one target sequence.
    But while zooming in in a macroscopic scale it’s a relatively easy experience, in a microscopic level is a whole different story, it takes the participation of an entire team! It is an extraordinary performance where several kinds of enzymes, nucleotides, primers, temperatures and many others come together to achieve one single goal.
    If you would like to call them the Avengers of the microscopic world you would be right to do so, because this assembling will grant the identification of pathogenic microorganisms, especially the ones that cause well known infectious diseases.
    As an engineer, by integrating this BEST course you will see in first-hand the action of this heroes in the shadows and participate directly in the PCR process. The field of Genetics is waiting for you, the field of Molecular Biology is waiting for you, the microscopic world is waiting for you!

    Don´t let them down!

About BEST Lisbon

    BEST is an apolitical, non-profit NGO in constant growth. More than organizing events for students, BEST strives to strengthen the ties between universities, students and companies.
    Formed in 1989, BEST now counts with over 3300 members, spread out across 34 countries in 96 Local BEST Groups (LBGs).
    Lisbon is one of the founding members of BEST, with its headquarters located in at Instituto Superior Tecnico, University of Lisbon.
    We are now counting with over 46 members, who have and will keep on delivering quality services to students from all around Europe.
    For more information about our LBG or BEST as an organization, go to our website or to BEST Internacional's website, respectively.
